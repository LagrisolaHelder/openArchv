# Privacy Policy for openArchv

The openArchv application is built as an open-source utility. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as-is.

## Information Collection and Use
openArchv runs entirely locally on your machine. 
* **No Personal Data:** The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.
* **No Tracking:** There are no analytics frameworks, tracking cookies, or third-party advertising networks embedded in the software.
* **Local Processing:** All file compression, decompression, and archive management processes happen exclusively on your local device.

## Contact
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to open an issue on the repository tracking page.
